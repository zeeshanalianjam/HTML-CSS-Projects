Admission Procedure

  • Admission shall be based on merit and merit, in turn, will be determined by the admission committee, as per laid down admission criteria.
  • If any candidate fails to appear before the admission committee for interview, he/she may be considered for admission with late fee payment.
  • If any of the particulars given by a candidate in his/her application for admission are found incorrect or false, his/her admission shall be cancelled.

Cancellation of Admission

The management of institute reserves the right to refuse / cancel the admission of the candidate at any stage, who has obtained his admission by making any miss-statement or concealing a material, fact particularly regarding his / her age, domicile, DMC or any other valid reason. Such cancellation shall be notified on the notice board and will be communicated to the candidate.

Appeal against such cancellation can be made to the institute appellate committee. The decision of the committee shall be final. If a student on roll remains absent from classes continuously for one week or if his/her attendance falls below 50% at the end of each month, without any reason for which prior permission is essential, the Principal shall cancel his admission.

Re-Admission & Migration

The admission cancelled may be restored when the institute administration is satisfied that the admission was cancelled due to circumstance beyond human control. Re-Admission fee of Rs. 500/- shall be charged and credited to the College Fund

Examination & Tests

The Board of Technical Education Peshawar has adopted terminal system of examination normally held in March & September of every year. Besides terminal examinations, two mid-term examinations will be held during the year. The results of these exams will be communicated to the parents / guardians. Students remain absent from test, fined heavily and may struck off in case of poor performance.

Attendance Requirements

Students are required to be regular in attendance 75% in aggregate and 60% attendance in each subject is binding on every student. Anyone failing to comply with this rule his/her examination form will not be sent to the Board of Technical Education Peshawar to sit form the terminal examination. No student can be excused from attendance rule except in case of serious illness or any other unavoidable circumstances.

Students Conduct & Discipline

Enrollment in Future Computers World & College of Vocational Training obligates the students to behave in a responsible and disciplined manner, both inside and outside the institute. Failure to do so will result in imposition of penalty, ranging from fine to rustication or expulsion from the Institute, in accordance with "Students Conduct & Discipline Regulations"
