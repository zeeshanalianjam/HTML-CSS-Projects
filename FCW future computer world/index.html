About FCW & CVT

Future Computers World & College of Vocational Training, Tank was firstly setup in 2000, to share the burden of Government for the improvement of technical education on one hand and to provide valued-based technical education to the people of this remote area of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa on other hand.

The goal of this college is to achieve a high-level of academic excellence, which measures favorably on the touchstone of international standards to build up human resources necessary for technical education devoted to teaching research & nations development.

This institute offers one year Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) and so many other short courses like Office Automation, Computerized Accountancy, Architectural Engineering (AutoCAD), Shorthand, Web Designing, General Electrician, Plumber, Quantity Survey, Civil Survey, Safety Management, Mobile Repairing, Computer Hardware & Many More... (on male side), while Computer Applications, Beautician, Hand Embroidery , Machine Embroidery etc (on female side)

The college has well equipped computer labs & technical workshops. The instructional force consits of highly qualified and sincere teachers divers experience and technical know-how. The patronage, management determined to convert this institution in an embodiment of excellence.

Mission Statment

To provide technical education to poor & needy people at their door step to both genders of highest quality, groom their technical skills & personality, enhance sense of responsibility, commitment, confidence & dedication towards their society & country.
